B Com Finance

BCom Finance is an undergraduate degree course spanning 3 to 4 academic years. This course incorporates the fundamental information and knowledge of a traditional BCom along with specialised subjects that deal with Finance, Banking and Investments.

focuses on the areas of accounting and financial analysis. With this unique blend, the programme enables students to propel their careers as wealth creators and managers both in corporate and merchant banking domains.provides students with a strong foundation in financial concepts and practices, particularly as they relate to the banking industry. Students pursuing BCom will be able to work in a variety of sectors and roles such as Accountant Manager, Finance Manager, Project Manager, Account Executive, Financial Adviser, Executive Assistant, Chartered Accountant.

Eligibility

Complete 10+2 or equivalent exam from a recognized board with a minimum aggregate of 50% marks The minimum marks required may vary from college to college
